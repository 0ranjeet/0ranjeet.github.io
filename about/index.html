I build systems that solve real operational problems. Not just software — but the underlying workflows, data structures, and automation that make businesses actually work better.

I started my journey working with SMEs who were drowning in spreadsheets, manual approvals, and disconnected tools. I watched owners become bottlenecks for their own growth. That's when I realized: most businesses don't need more features — they need better systems.

Today, I specialize in building custom ERP systems, AI automation workflows, and digital transformation solutions that eliminate friction. My approach: understand the actual work, design the right process, then write clean code that lasts.
